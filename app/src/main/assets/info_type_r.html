Properties of Thermocouple Type R

Materials2,1

Positive element Negative element
Platinum (87%), Rhodium (13%) Platinum (100%)


Effective Temperature Range1

Up to 1480°C for protected thermocouples of wire size 24 American wire gauge (AWG).

Recommended Uses1

For continuous use in oxidizing or inert atmospheres over the temperature range of 0 to 1480°C, or for short-term use in vacuum environment.
Not to be used in reducing atmospheres and environments containing metallic or nonmetallic vapors.
