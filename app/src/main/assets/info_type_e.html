Properties of Thermocouple Type E

Materials2,1

Positive element Negative element
Nickel (90%), Chromium (10%) Nickel (55%), Copper (45%)


Effective Temperature Range1

Up to 540°C for protected thermocouples of wire size 20 American wire gauge (AWG).
Up to 430°C for protected thermocouples of wire size 24 AWG.

Recommended Uses1

May be used in oxidizing and inert atmospheres over the temperature range of -200 to 900°C.
Resistant to corrosion in moist atmosphere, and suitable for subzero temperature measurements.
Not to be used in sulfurous, marginally oxidizing or reducing atmospheres.
