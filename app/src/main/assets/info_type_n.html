Properties of Thermocouple Type N

Materials2,1

Positive element Negative element
Nickel (84.4%), Chromium (14.2%), Silicon (1.4%) Nickel (95.5%), Silicon (4.4%), Manganese (0.15%)


Effective Temperature Range1

Up to 980°C for protected thermocouples of wire size 20 American wire gauge (AWG).
Up to 870°C for protected thermocouples of wire size 24 AWG.

Recommended Uses3

May be used in oxidizing and inert atmospheres over the temperature range of -270 to 1300°C.
