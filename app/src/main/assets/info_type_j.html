Properties of Thermocouple Type J

Materials2,1

Positive element Negative element
Iron (99.5%)* Nickel (55%), Copper (45%)

* Thermoelectric iron contains trace amount of Carbon, Manganese, Sulfur, Phosphorus, Silicon, Nickel, Copper and Chromium.

Effective Temperature Range1

Up to 480°C for protected thermocouples of wire size 20 American wire gauge (AWG).
Up to 370°C for protected thermocouples of wire size 24 AWG.

Recommended Uses1

May be used in vacuum, oxidizing, reducing and inert atmospheres over the temperature range of 0 to 760°C.
Not to be used in sulfurous atmospheres above 540°C.
